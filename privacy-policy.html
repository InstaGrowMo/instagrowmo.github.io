Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how InstaGrowMo collects, uses, and protects information when you interact with our website and services.

Information Collection

Please be aware that **our website itself is static and does not employ cookies or other tracking technologies to collect your personal Browse data.**

Personal information is **only collected when you voluntarily provide it to us by initiating contact and sending your order details via WhatsApp messaging.** This information may include your name, the Instagram post/reel/video link or profile link you provide, the desired quantity of service, and any specific comments or instructions related to your order.

Use of Information

The information you provide to us through WhatsApp is used **solely for the purpose of processing, fulfilling, and communicating with you regarding your specific service order.**

We do not use your information for marketing purposes unless you explicitly consent during our WhatsApp communication.

Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others for their marketing purposes.

We may share the necessary information (such as the Instagram link and desired quantity) with our trusted service providers only to the extent required to fulfill your order. We ensure that these providers are bound by confidentiality obligations.

We may also disclose information if legally required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information collected through WhatsApp communications against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, please note that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Data Retention

We retain the information collected via WhatsApp only for as long as necessary to fulfill your order and provide customer support related to that order. Once the service is completed and any support period has passed, we will take steps to securely delete or anonymize your information.

Your Rights

Since data collection occurs primarily through WhatsApp communication initiated by you, your rights regarding access, correction, or deletion of your personal information are exercised by contacting us directly via our WhatsApp number.

Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to other websites, such as Instagram. Please be aware that we are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such other sites. We encourage our users to be aware when they leave our site and to read the privacy statements of any other site that collects personally identifiable information.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us via WhatsApp, and we will take steps to remove that information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the WhatsApp number provided on our website.
